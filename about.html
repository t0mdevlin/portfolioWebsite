Hi, I'm Tom. I am a full time Software Engineer working in the Telecoms industry. This is my personal portfolio website where you can get to know me better.

I have been working in the Telecoms industry for a little under three years now. Prior to that I studied at TUS (Formerly Athlone Institute of Technology) from which I earned my BSc. in Cloud Computing and MSc. in Applied Software Engineering. Since then I have worked in a variety of different roles within my current employment.

The first role I took on after college was working as a staging engineer for the companies products. Before release the products would go through a series of testing loops in an automated pipeline. It was my job to ensure these pipelines ran smoothly and if any bugs were to arise it would be reported back to the design teams for prompt fixing. Aside from this it was also my responsibility to take in new use case scenarios, for new features added to the products and implement these as part of the testing pipeline.
